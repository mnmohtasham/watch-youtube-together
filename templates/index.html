    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    Is DittoPlay really free?

    Yes! DittoPlay is 100% free to use for hosting a watch party. We created this tool to make it easy for friends and long-distance family to watch YouTube videos together, no matter where they are.

    Do my friends need to install anything?

    No, there is absolutely nothing to install. DittoPlay works directly in your web browser (like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari) on both desktop and mobile devices.
